[[sec-beaufortForce]]
=== Beaufort Force
[cols="a",options="headers"]
|===
a|[underline]#**IHO Definition** :# Wind force scale, originally based on the state of the sea, expressed in numbers from 0 to 12, as defined by WMO (WMO 306 Manual on Codes, Volume I.1 - International Codes). + 
[underline]#** Attribute Type :**# enumeration + 
99) calm + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Absence of air motion or wind with a speed of less than 1 knot (Beaufort scale wind force 0). + 
1) light air + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 1 and 3 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 1) + 
2) light breeze + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 4 and 6 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 2). + 
3) gentle breeze + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 7 and 10 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 3). + 
4) moderate breeze + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 11 and 16 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 4). + 
5) fresh breeze + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 17 and 21 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 5). + 
6) strong breeze + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 22 and 27 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 6). + 
7) near gale + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 28 and 33 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 7). + 
8) gale + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 34 and 40 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 8). + 
9) strong gale + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 41 and 47 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 9). + 
10) storm + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 48 and 55 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 10). + 
11) violent storm + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Wind with a speed between 56 and 63 knots (Beaufort scale wind force 11). + 
12) hurricane + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :(1) Term, derived from a Caribbean word, first applied to tropical cyclones of the Caribbean Sea. (2) Wind with a speed 64 knots or higher (Beaufort scale wind force 12). + 
 
[underline]#** Remarks :**# Note: for S-412, we will limit the attribute to enumeration values 7-12. + 
|===

[cols="a",options="headers"]
|===
a|[underline]#**IHO Definition** :# The type of weather phenomenon causing hazardous ice accretion. + 
[underline]#** Attribute Type :**# enumeration + 
1) freezingSpray + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Sea spray transported through air at temperatures below 0C. + 
2) freezingRain + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Precipitation drops freezing on impact to form a coating of clear ice (glaze) on the ground and on exposed objects. + 
3) freezingFog + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Tiny, supercooled liquid water droplets in fog can freeze instantly on exposed surfaces when surface temperatures are at or below freezing. + 
4) otherIcingSource + 
[underline]#**IHO Definition**# :Source of icing is unknown or is different from other types of icing sources. +
